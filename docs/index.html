How to subscribe

iPhone / iPad / Mac → tap “Subscribe” → Add. (Mac: File ▸ New Calendar Subscription, paste the URL.)

Google Calendar (desktop) → Other calendars ▸ + ▸ From URL → paste the feed URL → Add. Then it syncs to the app on Android/iPhone.

Outlook → Add calendar ▸ Subscribe from web → paste the URL.

Notes

  • BBC = iPlayer, live in 4K UHD | ITV = ITVX, HD only (STV in Scotland) - the channel name alone tells you 4K vs HD.
  • 🇯🇵 NHK (NHK総合), NTV (日本テレビ), Fuji (フジテレビ) = terrestrial free-to-air | BS4K = NHK BS Premium 4K only.
  • Only DAZN carries all 104 live in Japan (paid) - matches with no Japanese free channel are DAZN-only.
  • Group stage is fully labelled. Knockouts: the final is on BBC + ITV; England's & Scotland's games follow the BBC (R32/R16/SF) / ITV (QF) routing and auto-fill once their opponent is known; neutral ties show bracket slots like 1A v 3C/E/F/H/I until confirmed.
  • ⏱️ Kickoffs are stored in UTC and shown in your device's own time zone.
