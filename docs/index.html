What the labels mean

  • 🇬🇧 BBC / ITV — the UK free-to-air broadcaster (also on iPlayer / ITVX; STV in Scotland).
  • 🇯🇵 NHK (NHK総合), NTV (日本テレビ), Fuji (フジテレビ) — Japanese free-to-air. BS4K = NHK BS Premium 4K only.
  • No 🇯🇵 label = DAZN only in Japan (paid) — only DAZN carries all 104 live.
  • Group stage is fully labelled. Knockout broadcasters are TBC (they depend on who qualifies) and show bracket slots like 1A v 3C/E/F/H/I until teams are confirmed.
  • ⏱️ Every kickoff is stored in UTC and shown in your device's own time zone automatically.
